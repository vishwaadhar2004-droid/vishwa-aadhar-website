Admin Instructions

Welcome to the Vishwa Aadhar Admin Panel. This simplified interface allows you to manage the most active parts of your website:

  • Team Members: Add, edit, or remove members of your organization. These appear on the "About Us" page.
  • Videos: Link YouTube or Instagram videos to showcase your work. These appear on the "Videos" page.

Any changes you save here will be reflected Live on the public website within seconds.
